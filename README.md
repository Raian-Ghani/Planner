The Core Loop: Work for Rewards
The blueprint starts simple: you plug in your active tasks and your favorite hobbies. But instead of just checking a box, Mission Control introduces a high-stakes gamification loop:

AI Proof-of-Work Verification: When you finish a task, you submit your proof. An integrated AI model analyzes your submission to verify that you actually put in the real work.

The Dual-Slot Machine: Passing the AI verification layer triggers a custom two-sided slot machine:

Slot 1 (The Wildcard): A completely randomized reward or hobby access token.

Slot 2 (The AI Time-Sizer): An AI-calculated time limit tailored precisely to how well you executed, the task's priority level, and the physical time you spent building.

The Economy of Focus
The "Use It or Lose It" Protocol: Once you unlock a reward, you can cash it in immediately or bank it for later. Choose wisely: banked standard rewards expire in exactly 7 days.

The High-Priority Break: Crushing a maximum-priority objective triggers a "High Fun, High Time" reward tier. These ultra-premium tokens give you extended hobby access but self-destruct within 8 days if left unused.

AI Companion Interface: To keep the slot machine evolving and unpredictable, a custom AI interface lets you talk about your hobbies, workflows, or ideas (hardcoded with absolute safety protocols, of course). This continuously recalibrates the reward variations to keep your focus loop fresh.
